Lifehack

1. any procedure or action that solves a problem, simplifies a task, reduces frustration, etc, in one's everyday life verb

2. to live one's life in a way such as to simplify tasks and minimize problems

LIFEHACK EXAMPLE

What I made after learning lifehack

Wrap your cheese in parchment paper

Did you know that cheese lasts longer when it can 'breathe'? Rather than wrapping your cheddar up in cling film or tin foil, secure it in waxed or greaseproof paper. (You might want to put it in a food container to stop it from stinking out your food in the fridge).

Fold and store bedding inside a pillowcase

This lifehack for life will keep your clean bedding tidy and has the added benefit of keeping them together. No more searching around for a loose pillowcase..

Put lemon juice on your avocado and guacamole to make it keep for longer

No one wants a browning avocado or guacamole. The key to keeping it green is squeezing some lemon or lime juice over it. The citric acid will prevent it from going brown for longer and adds an extra freshness to the flavor.
